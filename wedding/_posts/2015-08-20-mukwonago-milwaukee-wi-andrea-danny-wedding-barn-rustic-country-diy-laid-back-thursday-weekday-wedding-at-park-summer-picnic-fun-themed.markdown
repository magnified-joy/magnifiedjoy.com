Andrea + Danny didn't want to have their wedding on Saturday and Sunday because it's sabbath. Didn't like the idea of Friday as an alternative, so why not a Thursday wedding?!  They have dedicated their lives and their relationship to Jesus Christ and want their wedding day to represent truth, purity, and, above all else, love. With this being said, their wedding vision is highly laid back, rustic, bohemian, country, but yet classy fun barn party. There are hay bales, picnic blankets, picnic tables, mason jars, wild flowers, yard games, tractor rides, covered wagon rides, and lots of visiting and laughter. 

John 15:13  Greater love has no one than this, than to lay down one’s life for his friends.
