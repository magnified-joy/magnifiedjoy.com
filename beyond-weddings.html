Our work goes beyond weddings and engagements.

We work with small-scale artisans and craftsmen to offer beautiful touches to your photo experience.

For example, the MJP keepsake box is hand crafted by our friend and past groom, talented woodcraftsman Adam Gunderson, made with sustainably harvested wood and engraved with our logo. It is perfect for your engagement, boudoir, and wedding images, as well as love notes, jewelry and other keepsakes

Your wedding supports a good cause.

MJP supports Weddings for Freedom to fight back against modern day slavery. Along with wedding vendors across the country, we annually donate a portion of our proceeds towards rescuing girls and boys from slavery and providing them with a safe haven and resources. A portion of the proceeds from YOUR wedding goes toward supporting this critical cause and has effects wider than we can imagine.
