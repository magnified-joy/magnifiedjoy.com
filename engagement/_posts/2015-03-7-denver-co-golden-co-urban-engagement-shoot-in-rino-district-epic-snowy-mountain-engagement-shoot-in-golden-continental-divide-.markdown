We got to know Dana + Jhun through our church here in Denver. They are amazing individuals, we can only imagine how amazing these two will become as a power team! When we asked them where they wanted their engagement shoot, Jhun really wanted an urban look, while Dana’s heart was calling her to the mountains. We spent half our time in RINO district in Denver, then we drove out to Golden toward the Continental Divide Overlook (unfortunately we couldn’t make it to the overlook in our humble Prius), so we just looked for trails with mountainous landscapes to do the second half of our shoot.
