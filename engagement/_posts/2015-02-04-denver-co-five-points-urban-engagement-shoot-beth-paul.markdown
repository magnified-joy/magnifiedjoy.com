Beth + Paul are both artists in their own ways. Beth is an awesome yoga instructor, and Paul draws and make <a href="http://mountainvsplains.com/">witty cards that are sold all around Denver</a>. He also painted the mural that we photographed them in front of. They chose to have their engagement session at Five Points because that was the neighborhood where they first met. Beth + Paul are real troopers, we took them to alleyways and shot by dumpsters and they always have full smiles and emotions!
