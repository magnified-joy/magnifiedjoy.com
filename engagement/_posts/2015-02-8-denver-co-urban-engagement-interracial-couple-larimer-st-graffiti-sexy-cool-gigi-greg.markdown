Before we photograph our couples, we like to ask them what three words they each would use to describe their relationship or personalities. Gigi + Greg chose the words fun, edgy, sincere, and sexy cool. We think these photos totally reflect their adjectives. The street corners, alleyways, and graffiti murals around Larimer St. were the perfect setting for their shoot. We had a great time hanging out and cracking jokes! If you ever meet Gigi + Greg, ask them to tell you the joke about the donut hole.
