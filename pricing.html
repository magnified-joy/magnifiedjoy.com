Pricing

MJP offers two pricing options: Packages and A la Carte

  1. Packages start at $4,700 and include the following:
    • A complimentary 2-hour engagement shoot. This gives us a chance to get to know you as a couple, and for you to see how Maria and En Tao work together as a team so that you are completely comfortable on your wedding day. In addition, you will have beautiful photos to use for your guestbook.
    • Artisan guestbook with your engagement photos
    • UNLIMITED coverage on your wedding day. We will not charge you by the hour. We will stay with you from getting ready in the morning until the party ends, so that we are sure to capture each and every special moment. When you book your wedding with us, the entire day is yours!
    • Both En Tao and Maria shooting your wedding day, so as to capture multiple angles and not miss a shot
    • A handmade wooden keepsake box
    • An average 2 week turnaround time on your wedding images
    • An online gallery where you and your friends and family can view photos and order copies.
    • A high resolution USB with your images for printing and sharing.
    • A set price for your wedding anywhere in the 50 states!
    • And more! Contact us for full details.
  2. We also arrange a la carte packages for non-traditional weddings, shorter time frames, and international events. Contact us with your ideas and we will work with you to customize as you need.

A Note About Payment

A retainer is due when you return your signed contract. Your date is secured when we have received this retainer and signed contract. We prefer checks, but a credit card option is also available.
